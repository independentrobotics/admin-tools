ISED workflow

ISED Claim Generator

Builds formatted employee timesheets from a Notion time log export. This is the original tool for turning employee work logs into the structured workbook used for ISED-style claim submissions.

  • Upload a Notion CSV export
  • Set billing period and grouping options
  • Generate one formatted timesheet per employee

OSC workflow

OSC Claim Generator

Computes claimable employee totals from Notion time logs, wage and benefits data, and remaining work-package balances. It also lets you allocate billable amounts by work package and export both the claim output and the updated remaining balances.

  • Filter by date range and billing code
  • Allocate employee totals across eligible work packages
  • Export claim workbook and updated remaining balances
